06 / THE FIGHTBACK

Stop focusing on
what to quit.

The biggest mistake in recovery is making "stopping" the goal. Stopping is a side-effect of building. Here is what to build.

01

Start with the Body, Not the Mind

Your body is the fastest route to your brain. Before journaling, before podcasts, before strategies — do twenty push-ups. Walk. Run. Lift. Physical action interrupts the pattern faster than any thought will.

Go now: Use the Health Hub above → find your nearest gym in one click
02

Understand It Was Never Real

What you consumed was a produced, scripted, manufactured illusion. The "people" you watched were performing, often under contracts they didn't fully understand, for companies profiting from your dopamine. God's creation — real human connection — looks nothing like that.

Read: "Your Brain on Porn" — Gary Wilson. Start with Chapter 2.
03

Audit Your Environment Ruthlessly

Delete. Uninstall. Reconfigure. Your phone is an environment. Design it the way you'd design your room — deliberately. Remove every shortcut to the habit.

Tool: BlockSite (Chrome) · Cold Turkey (PC) · Screen Time (iOS) · Digital Wellbeing (Android)
04

Replace the Stimulus, Don't Just Block It

Your brain needs stimulation. It will seek it. If you remove the source without providing an alternative, the old pathway wins. The gym, creative work, sport, community — these produce real dopamine without the crash.

Framework: Identity shift — you're not "quitting porn." You're becoming someone who doesn't need it.
05

Ground Yourself Spiritually

Every faith tradition has teachings on this — and they predate neuroscience by millennia. Prayer, discipline, community, fasting, service — these are not superstitions. They are tested technologies for human flourishing.

Start: Find a religious community near you — see the Community section above
06

Give It Back — Share What You Know

The final stage is transmission. You don't need to be fully healed to help someone else. You just need to be honest about the road. That honesty is more powerful than any campaign.

That's why: This website exists. Share this page with someone who needs it.
